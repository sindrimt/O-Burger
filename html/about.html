OUR FOOD

We’re passionate about our food and take great care in ensuring that our customers are served safe and delicious food of the highest quality. Food safety and quality is very important to us and will always be at the core of everything we do, from farm to fork.

We use real, quality ingredients to elevate the taste of the our food and the spirits of people who enjoy it all over the world (e.g we use 100 % pure beef in our burgers). Our meat comes from humane farms, where the animals are handled with great care from birth to slaughter.

Behind the scenes, we’re working hard to continously improve how we source our ingredients, ensuring the best possible result for the people, animals and the planet.


O'STORY

O’Burger is an American fast casual restaurant chain based in Honolulu. It started out as a small burger stand outside of the Ala Moana Center, solely driven by former President Barack Obama. The stand quickly gathered traction for its notoriously tasty Hawaiian burgers. However, in the summer of 2019, the stand was moved inside of the Ala Moana Center, and transformed into a restaurant. Following this transition, the menu expanded vastly – now serving fried chicken, nuggets, and its renowned milkshakes.

The idea of O’Burger was first conceived back in 2011, during Obama’s first period of presidency. Considering he was the President of the United States at the time; he decided to put the idea to the side. After his eight years of presidency, Obama could finally focus on his long-lost childhood dream.

Since its founding, it has opened six restaurants, where five of them are located along the West Coast of America. Regarding Americans who live on the East Coast; they have no need to fear. O’Burger has already scheduled the opening of four more restaurants, which all are spread along the Atlantic Coast. Everyone should be granted the chance to enjoy O'burger, no matter where one may live.
