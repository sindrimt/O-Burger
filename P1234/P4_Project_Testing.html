Testing Audience:

As briefly mentioned above, our current situation has made it difficult to perform live testing. Therefore, we decided to distribute the link to the website along with link to the Google Form through social media posts and direct messages. Naturally, by using this method, some of our friends, family or colleagues become testers, which may or may not have some bias. Despite these problems, we believe our testers more or less fitted our intended audience.
